Dr Soil Health

ORGANIC

Dr. Soil Health is an innovation in biotechnology, a unique product formulated by SBTL and marketed by Way2Agribusiness India Pvt Ltd. Dr. Soil Health is manufactured using plant extracts which supplements energy source for higher yield and harvest. Dr Soil Health is composed of natural earthworm exudates, seaweeds extract, amino acids, vitamins, Fulvic acids, humic acids, chelated micro and macro nutrients, which are derived from natural plant origin. Dr Soil Health is very rich in micronutrients. Dr. Soil Health will result in development of roots & shoots and stimulates the cell division, improves the crop size and increasing the beneficial micro-organisms. Dr Soil Health promotes organic / natural crop cultivation practices in place of conventional chemical practices. One of the favourable developments in agriculture sector is farmers are gaining awareness that they have to enrich soil fertility and thereby get higher yield but not by soil exploitation through chemical practices. After application of Dr Soil Health, one can notice desirable progress such as soil turning soft, growth of earthworm, new roots formation, good vegetative growth of crops and finally higher yields.
